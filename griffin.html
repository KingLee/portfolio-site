Simply the best in fall protection.

A project while working with O3 World.

Rigid Lifelines started a series of sub-brands with The Griffin being their first exploration into this new territory. Taking one of their top selling items, the Portable Monorail, and rebranding it as The Griffin. Sounds much better doesn't it? The goal of this project was to give their field sales team (armed with iPads) a tool that clearly and professionaly explained the benefits of the product. Not only does the site work for off-site sales, but it proves an invaluable resource at trade shows.

A dead simple interface.

The best thing about this site is the ease of use. Highlights are clearly called out to show videos in key areas. The videos associated with each type of vehicle are surprisingly fun to watch and clearly show the benefits of the system. To navigate to the next section, you have big buttons with arrows to move through the screens. There is a low chance of a user getting lost within the content, and it presents the pitch in a logical sequence.

A sprinkle of animation.

We decided to include some very subtle animations to catch the attention of users and potential customers. The circle screen takeover is a neat effect that allows us to present The Griffin separate from the problems of other systems displayed on white. We also include a few transitions elsewhere that ehance the idea that the griffin is portable and works for many different applications.
