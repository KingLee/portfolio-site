Who says you can't teach an old dog new tricks? Sherman, now on Tumblr.

A project while working with O3 World.

According to Sherman is a project near and dear to my heart. The concept is that a dog gives advice to humans on how to be more human, and of course he needs a blog for it. Regardless of what people say, this has been some of the most fun I've ever had designing a website. The Tumblr design is full of dog puns both visually and in copy.

Modular comic themed... theme.

The content was supposed to take a turn towards a daily comic strip style of humor. I took the idea of an old 50's comic book and took it to an extreme. If you ever see a comic book in real life that is this yellow... I suggest you stay away. I used subtle patterns, custom icons, comic-chic typography and gratuitous use of color, and I loved every bit of it. I designed all the modules to be around 280px wide, making all the content contained within them, ready for mobile. We used masonry to be able to arrange and call in blocks with ease.

Bold icons made for dogs.
