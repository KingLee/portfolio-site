An Overview of Goal Investor

A project while working with O3 World.

Goal Investor is a platform that allows users to see how much money it will take to achieve long term goals such as retirement, or a child's education costs. These goals are often hard to predict. With this tool, you get personalized financial insights from one of the most successful wealth management companies.

Easy does not make Enjoyable.

One of the things we struggled with, was the emotional impact of having discussions about retirement or large dollar figure expenses. We tried to soften some of these harder emotional topics through our design decisions and copywriting. Just because something is easy to use, does not necessarily mean it is fun to use. As the designer, I wanted the interface to feel soft, tactile, and playful, to soften the subject matter.

Are your financial goals on track? How we show progress.

We have been continually making upgrades to Goal Investor. After some usability testing, we heard a few interesting things. One being, the way we were showing goal progress was confusing. We rethought this area of the site and came up with the progress gauge to more clearly show the percentage of the goal that you will achieve.

Break out the pen and paint.

One of my first tasks on Goal Investor was to take wireframes and translate them into a welcoming and sophisticated interface. I decided to apply subtle textures and gradients to partly convey that feeling. Color palette also played a big role in helping guide the emotions of the users. Finally, we decided to use illustrations throughout the site, as opposed to photos. We felt that this would greatly contribute to a more approachable experience when tackling such stressful subject matter.
