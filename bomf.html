According to Sherman

Hitting the ground running on a new responsive website.

A project while working with O3 World.

Back On My Feet is a non profit organization that helps those experiencing homelessness change the way they see themselves and hopes to help them get "back on their feet" through the power of running and strict routine. The end goal is to help these people find employment and independent living.

The center of information for all things Back On My Feet.

There is a large amount of information on the site. Information about the history, the process, partners, an events calendar, running teams and their members, how to volunteer, the ability to donate... and the list goes on. My main task was to style the UI with textures and colors, photo assets, icons, and typography to help digest this massive amount of information in all its forms.

Appreciate the past, look towards the future.

One of the most unique parts of the site is the timeline. Visually, I consider it to be interesting, but the best part is that the content is deserving of the visuals. Reading through the backstory of the company is something you could get caught up in for a while.
