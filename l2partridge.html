Emphasis on collaboration.

A project while working with O3 World.

L2Partridge is a Philadelphia architecture and interior design firm that stands by the principles of collaboration. They have a highly communicative process between their architects, construction project managers, developers, brokers, and end-users and needed a site to display their work and explain their process.

Building responsive, pun intended.

L2Partridge wanted a responsive build from day one. We had to consider all the features they were asking for and how they would translate to a mobile setting. We relied on a few image fallbacks in certain tricky areas, but for the most part, everything translated without a hitch.

Forward thinking techincally and in practice.

Not only am I excited by a client who wants and understands the need for a responsive site, but to see forward thinking in their practice as well, is refreshing. One of the most important stats around L2Partridge is that they have 50% of the LEED-CI projects in the Philadelphia area, giving them some great expereience dealing with future-energy-friendly buildings. We created some small stats graphics to display some of their achievements, as well as their collaboration process.
